About Me

I am a passionate music educator dedicated to creating meaningful and engaging learning experiences for my students. With over 10 years of experience in choral music, my approach to education has been shaped to blend foundational training with new, creative methods to learning. My goal is to foster a classroom environment where confidence is built, creativity is praised, and love for music can thrive. I believe that music has the power to create beautiful memories, build community, and positively impact students far beyond the classroom. As an educator, I aim to make learning both fun and purposeful, allowing students to grow not only as musicians but as individuals. Through encouragement, meaningful relationships, and intentional teaching, I aim to inspire an appreciation for music while leaving a lasting, positive impression on every student I teach.
